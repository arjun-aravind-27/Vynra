FAQ

Is this a chatbot or a human service?

It’s an AI assistant configured for your gym. It answers common questions, books sessions, and escalates to your team when needed.

Do I need new software?

No. We plug into the tools you already use. If you don’t have a booking tool, we’ll set up a simple one for you.

Can you build other automations (e.g., lane reservations)?

Yes. Many clubs start with the receptionist and add flows like lane reservations, waitlists, onboarding, billing nudges, or post‑visit surveys. We scope these during a short intake.

What about accuracy, tone, and privacy?

We train on your policies, pricing ranges, and voice. Logs are reviewed to refine responses. We only collect the data needed to book and follow up, and you can request deletion at any time.

Who owns the accounts?

We can host under our licenses for speed, or set up under yours. Either way, you retain access to your data.

How much does it cost?

Most gyms start under $1k setup with a light monthly plan ($250–$500). See pricing above for ballparks.
